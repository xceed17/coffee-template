ABOUT

Distance must not be a barrier to spreading knowledge. Transcending geographical boundaries, Xceed serves as miniature version of Kurukshetra encompassing interesting events and workshops. Apart from bagging lots of prizes, the winners of Xceed get the enviable opportunity to be a part of the grand final stage at Kurukshetra. Quite simply, XCEED provides those beyond our state borders the chance to experience Kurukshetra first hand.

Since 2008, XCEED has visited some of the top institutions in our country. Listed below are some of them in the order of their ranking.

  • Indian Institute of Technology –Kanpur
  • Indian Institute of Management –Ahmedabad, Bangalore and Kozhikode
  • College of Engineering –Delhi, Hyderabad and Pune
  • National Institute of Technology –Calicut, Warangal and Trichy
